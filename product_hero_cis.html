HERO Rebraning

To establish a clear brand image, HERO used a redesigned logo, adjusted CIS, and established guidelines. I tried many different directions during the design phase. I solicited feedbacks from colleagues and collected their anonymous responses, in which they described their feelings when they saw the design. I made adjustments based on their feedbacks. We finally selected an image that most easily expresses the idea of video learning, fast, and professional.
