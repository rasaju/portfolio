HERO App －Android/iOS

Now you can, not only memorize vocabulary or play a simple game, but learn a complete English lesson on mobile. Smart system will automatically arrange review schedule for you to enforce your learning. There are many functions such as notes, recordings, and flash cards to help you learn deeply and effectively.
